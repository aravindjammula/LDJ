Why Choose a Solitaire?

A solitaire setting is the purest way to showcase a diamond's natural beauty. By focusing on a single stone, the quality of the cut, color, and clarity takes center stage.

Certified Brilliance

All our solitaire diamonds above 0.50ct come with GIA or IGI certification to guarantee their quality.

Timeless Design

The solitaire style has remained the most popular choice for engagement rings for over a century.

Lifetime Warranty

We stand by the craftsmanship of every piece, offering a lifetime warranty and free annual cleaning.
