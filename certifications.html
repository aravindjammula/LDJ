BIS Hallmark Certification

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) hallmark is the government's guarantee of purity in gold jewellery. As a BIS-licensed jeweller, every piece of gold jewellery from LDJ Gold & Diamonds carries this official certification mark.

Our hallmark includes four essential symbols: the BIS logo, purity in karats, assaying center mark, and jeweller's identification mark. This comprehensive marking system provides complete transparency about the gold content in your jewellery.

Government-Regulated Purity

BIS hallmarking is mandated by the Government of India, ensuring standardized purity testing across all licensed jewellers.

Third-Party Verification

Independent BIS-approved assaying centers test each piece, providing unbiased verification of gold purity.

Complete Traceability

Each hallmark includes codes that allow complete traceability back to the assaying center and manufacturing date.

Our Quality Assurance Process

Beyond third-party certifications, we implement a rigorous internal quality assurance process that every piece must pass before it reaches our clients.

Our quality standards are informed by three generations of jewellery expertise, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern testing methods.

1

Material Verification

All metals and gemstones are verified for authenticity and quality upon receipt, with documentation maintained for traceability.

2

Craftsmanship Inspection

Each piece undergoes multiple inspections during creation, focusing on structural integrity, finish quality, and design accuracy.

3

Final Quality Check

Before presentation, each piece is examined under magnification for any imperfections and tested for durability and wear comfort.

4

Documentation & Certification

Complete documentation including certificates, care instructions, and warranty information is prepared for each piece.
