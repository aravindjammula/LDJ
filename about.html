For over three generations, LDJ Gold & Diamonds has been synonymous with exceptional craftsmanship, timeless design, and unwavering integrity in the world of fine jewellery.

Our Heritage

A family legacy of excellence in jewellery craftsmanship that spans over half a century

Three Generations of Master Craftsmanship

Founded in 1965 by Louis D. Johnson, our story began in a small workshop in New York's Diamond District. With nothing but his exceptional skill, a passion for perfection, and a vision to create jewellery that would stand the test of time, Louis laid the foundation for what would become a family legacy.

Louis believed that jewellery should tell a storyâ€”not just of the materials and craftsmanship, but of the moments and people it represents. This philosophy became the cornerstone of our business, guiding every piece we create to this day.

1965

The Beginning

Louis D. Johnson establishes LDJ Gold & Diamonds in a small workshop, specializing in custom engagement rings and heirloom redesigns.

1982

Second Generation

Louis's son, Michael Johnson, joins the business, bringing modern design sensibilities while preserving traditional craftsmanship techniques.

2005

Third Generation

Sophia Johnson, Louis's granddaughter, becomes Creative Director, blending contemporary design with the family's legacy of excellence.

Our Guiding Principles

The values that have guided our family business for over 50 years

Our Mission

To create exquisite, heirloom-quality jewellery that celebrates life's most significant moments while upholding the highest standards of craftsmanship, ethical sourcing, and personalized service that our family has maintained for generations.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in fine jewellery, recognized globally for our uncompromising quality, timeless designs, and commitment to preserving the art of traditional jewellery making for future generations.

Our Values

Integrity in every transaction, excellence in every detail, respect for tradition while embracing innovation, and a deep commitment to our clients' satisfaction and trust that extends far beyond the initial purchase.
