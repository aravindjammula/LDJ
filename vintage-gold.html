A Legacy of Excellence Since 1965

Founded by Louis D. Johnson with a vision to create jewellery that would stand the test of time, LDJ Gold & Diamonds has been a family-owned business for over three generations. Our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and personal service remains unchanged.

Each piece in our collection tells a story - of skilled artisanship, of precious materials ethically sourced, and of the moments that matter most in our clients' lives.

We believe that true luxury lies in the details: the perfect cut of a diamond, the weight of gold in your hand, and the knowledge that your jewellery will be treasured for generations to come.
