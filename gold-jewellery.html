Our Gold Varieties

Discover the different types of gold we work with, each offering unique characteristics and beauty

22K Gold

The purest form of gold used in jewellery, containing 91.6% pure gold. Known for its rich, deep yellow color and traditional appeal.

Purity: 91.6% Pure Gold Color: Rich Yellow Best For: Traditional & Heirloom Pieces

18K Gold

The perfect balance of purity and durability, containing 75% pure gold. Ideal for intricate designs and everyday wear.

Purity: 75% Pure Gold Color: Warm Yellow Best For: Everyday & Contemporary Designs

Rose Gold

18K gold alloyed with copper for its distinctive pink hue. A modern choice with romantic appeal and unique warmth.

Purity: 75% Pure Gold Color: Romantic Pink Best For: Modern & Romantic Pieces

Caring for Your Gold Jewellery

Proper care ensures your gold jewellery maintains its beauty and value for generations. Follow these expert tips to keep your pieces looking their best.

Regular Cleaning

Clean your gold jewellery with a soft cloth and mild soap solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage the surface.

Proper Storage

Store each piece separately in soft pouches to prevent scratches. Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight.

Professional Maintenance

Bring your jewellery to us annually for professional cleaning, inspection, and any necessary repairs.
