Our Design Process

A collaborative journey from initial concept to finished masterpiece. We guide you through every step to ensure your vision becomes reality.

Initial Consultation

We begin with a personal conversation to understand your vision, inspiration, and story. Bring sketches, photos, or ideas—or start from scratch with our designers' guidance.

Duration: 60-90 minutes In-person or virtual Bring inspiration materials

Concept Development

Our designers create initial sketches and digital renderings based on our discussion. We explore design options, materials, and gemstone selections.

Design Refinement

We refine the chosen concept based on your feedback. You'll see detailed drawings, material samples, and precise specifications before we begin crafting.
